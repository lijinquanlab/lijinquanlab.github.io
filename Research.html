Research & Publication

    Research Fields

    We mainly focus on developing phage-based methods of controlling antibiotic-resistant pathogens. We also work on molecular pathogenic mechanisms and population genomics of the pathogens. The PI was selected for the Outstanding Research Talents Development Program, Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

     

  1. Phage bank of zoonotic bacteria and production technology of therapeutic phage

    2.  

  2. New functional phage (tumor microenvironment, treatment of metabolic diseases)

    3.  

  3. High-throughput screening of novel phage lysin or phage-derived peptide based on genomics technology

    4.  

  4. Molecular mechanism of pathogenic bacteria breaking through the blood-brain barrier and mechanisms for the spread of pathogenic bacteria in the host
